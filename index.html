About Me

I work as a Digital Operations Support Coordinator at Kohl's Corporate Headquarters in Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin. I have an eclectic background with experience in both apparel product developement & SEO copy-writing before joining Digital Operations. I have a great passion for embracing diverse perspectives & ensuring everyone can bring their authentic selves to work. This has led to my most recent success at Kohl's, where I lead the Digital Diversity & Inclusion Task Force. I oversee five Chairs who collectively manage four committees dedicated to advancing diversity & inclusion for our associates, our customers, and our community. I am currently pursuing a Full Stack Web Development certification through the University of Minnesota.
